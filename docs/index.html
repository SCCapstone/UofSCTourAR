Tour History

Tour history shows you a list of stops you have visited. Similar to search, clicking on a stop gives you a brief summary about it.

Search

The search functionality of the application can be used to search for specific tour stops. This is useful if you don't know where to go next or you're away from campus and cannot access the AR functionality. Clicking on a stop will give you a brief description of the stop, it's location, and the date it was built.
